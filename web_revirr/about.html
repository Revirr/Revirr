What is Revirr?

Revirr is a job board consisting of only positions that require 0-2 years of experience. Entry-level positions are applicable to many kinds of people, at Revirr we focus on three.

The Graduate

Congratulations! School is over, but the hard part has yet to come. Create your resume and search for jobs that match your interests and skills.

Change Seeker

Have you been working on new skills and are ready to take the next step? Sharpen up that resume and apply to your new-found interests.

Military Transitions

Are you exiting the military and are ready to enter the civilian work force? Include veteran obtained experience on your resume to stand out.
